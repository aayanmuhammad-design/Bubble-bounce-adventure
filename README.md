# Bubble-bounce-adventure
Players guide the frog left and right, bouncing on moving or stationary bubbles to climb higher while collecting coins and avoiding hazards like bees or thorny bubbles. Reaching the top of the level within a time limit or before losing all hearts completes the stage and rewards points.
#Platform
The game will be made for desktop platforms—Windows, macOS, and Linux—because Pygame is a Python-based library designed primarily for desktop environments. It allows easy installation, supports 2D graphics efficiently, and offers keyboard/mouse controls ideal for kids.
#Visual style of game
The game is 2D side-on, showing the frog bouncing from bubble to bubble across a vertical pond. The world is bright and colorful, with floating bubbles, lily pads, and playful insects; the frog is green with big round eyes, a wide smile, and a tiny backpack for collecting coins.
#Assests required
The game will require images of the frog, bubbles, coins, lily pads, bees, and background scenery, all in bright, cartoon-style 2D sprites. It will also need music like cheerful, playful background tunes, and sound effects for jumping, collecting coins, landing on bubbles, and hitting hazards.
#Rules
The frog must stay on bubbles or platforms to avoid falling off the screen, and colliding with hazards like bees or thorny bubbles costs a heart. Players must reach the top of each level within the time limit, collecting as many coins and stars as possible without losing all hearts.
#Rewards
The player receives coins and stars for collecting items and completing levels successfully. Reaching the top of a level also earns points, extra hearts, or fun animations as rewards.
